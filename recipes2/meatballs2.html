This is the Meatball recipe

Ingredients

  • cocoa powder
  • Those lady cookies
  • Instant oatmeal
  • milk
  • Greek yogurt (vanilla or plain is fine)
  • espresso or instant coffee

Steps

  1. Prepare instant oatmeal according to instructions on the package
  2. Set aside
  3. Make coffee
  4. Mix coffee and oatmeal together
  5. Grab cup of your choice to assemble the Tiramisu Overnight Oatmeal
  6. Add the first layer of the coffee mixture at the bottom of your container
  7. Continue by adding the Greek yogurt as the second layer
  8. Continue until you've used all ingredients or have filled up your conntainer, but make sure the last layer is yogurt
  9. As the last layer, sprinkle cocoa powder heavily on top until you are satisfied
  10. Enjoy immediately or allow to chill overnight for the best results
